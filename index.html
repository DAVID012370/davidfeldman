About

Short bio that shows who Kianna is, what she sounds like, and what she loves creating. You can edit this text however you want.

Kianna Rain is a vocalist and songwriter blending intimate storytelling with cinematic, electronic production. Her voice sits right in the space between fragile and powerful – perfect for emotional drops, late-night house, dark pop and vibey soundtrack moments.


In the studio, she’s fast with melodies and harmonies, building full toplines, hooks and ad-libs that feel instantly alive. On stage, she brings a mix of raw emotion and confidence – connecting with the crowd while staying fully in the music.
