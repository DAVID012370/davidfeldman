About

A short overview of Kianna’s artistic universe. You can tweak or expand the text any time.

Kianna Rain is a multidisciplinary artist whose work flows between acting, music, painting and poetry — each discipline in conversation with the others. She approaches every role and performance with deep emotional intelligence, folding spiritual and psychological nuance into her characters.


As a singer, songwriter and producer, Kianna crafts soundscapes that blur the lines between pop, indie, rock and jazz. Her music often feels like both a confession and a dream — exploring love, consciousness, divinity and the unseen layers of being human.


Beyond the stage and studio, she paints and writes. Her canvases and poems echo the same themes found in her performances: the search for beauty, truth and transcendence.
